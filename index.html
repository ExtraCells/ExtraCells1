Welcome to Extra Cells.

This awesome AppliedEnergistics Add-On adds 4 bigger-sized storage cells, an adjustable storage cells and a special, multifunctional storage cell. A very special feature of the Mod is the Fluid Storage system it introduces. Thanks to it, you can store your fluids IN the ME-Network!
